Since 2001

The Hero of Time has Fought

In 2001, Young Link was added as a character in Super Smash Bros Melee. He is a clone of his older self, and differs in a number of ways: he is unsurprisingly smaller, weaker and lighter, yet boasts better mobility thanks to his much faster dashing speed, tether, and an ability to wall jump. He was added again in Super Smash Bros. Ultimate in 2018! Young Link relies on his fast speed, Kokiri sword, and projectiles to box in and combo the opponent.

This is a page for the Young Link Character Discord, where you can find resoures, guides, tips, matchmaking, spicy memes, joint matchups, and more!
